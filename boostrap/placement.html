What to Do After Graduation?

BCA (Bachelor in Computer Applications)

  • Pursue MCA (Master in Computer Applications)
  • Work as a Software Developer or Web Developer
  • Learn programming languages like Python, Java, or C++
  • Explore careers in Data Science or AI
  • Freelance as a Web Designer or App Developer

BBA (Bachelor in Business Administration)

  • Pursue an MBA (Master in Business Administration)
  • Start a career in Banking or Finance

  • Prepare for government exams like Lok Sewa Aayog
